Resource for Election Activity and Campaign Handling (REACH)

REACH is an innovative software designed by Green Apple Technologies & Systems, Inc., and it aims to provide intelligent solutions and streamlined process for the 2019 Philippine Elections.

Our software optimizes your platform through accurate and automated data gathering, to ensure convenience and integrity on voter targeting.

Designed for the holistic campaign and election experience, it provides tools that would last for the entire duration of your candidacy. Through gathering all the details about the voters for a specific area, and after cross-referencing across lists supplied by the government, search and tagging tools are mobilized.
Key Features
  • Logo of LGU
  • Photo and Signature of Resident
  • Complete Name
  • Blood Type
  • Date of Birth
  • Address and Barangay
  • QR Code
