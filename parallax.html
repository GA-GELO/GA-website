Our Products
MySmartGov
MySmartGOV is your LGU’s bridge to eReadiness success. Complete with the latest technology and a competent implementation and support team, the system modernizes the processing of government transactions providing for a streamlined, efficient and transparent delivery of public service.
Resident Information System
Resident Information System helps Local Government Units (LGUs) manage and maintain relevant information of their constituents. The system consists of a physical ID card given to each resident as well as a database system that maintains their relevant information.
Resource for Election Activity & Campaign Handling
Developed by Green Apple Technologies & Systems Inc., REACH presents a unique platform to identify and reach the target voting population. By segregating voters through different affiliations, a unique understanding of their behavior is presented.
Billing and Collection System
The Billing and Collection System is a customized system designed for Utility Concessionaires and Companies needing debt management and collections. It helps monitor and follow up delinquencies, as well as individual customer payments.
